# Chapters Page – Vanguard Capital League

Create an immersive **"Chapters"** page for the Vanguard Capital League website. This page should feel like exploring humanity's financial literacy network from space.

The page should be visually stunning, interactive, and highly engaging while remaining professional.

## Hero Experience

The page opens with a dark space background.

Centered on the screen is a beautiful interactive 3D Earth that slowly rotates.

The Earth should display glowing pins representing every official Vanguard chapter around the world.

Users can rotate and zoom the globe naturally.

The experience should feel smooth and premium.

Above the globe, display a headline similar to:

**Explore the Vanguard Network**

Underneath:

*"Every chapter begins on Earth. Every mission reaches beyond it."*

---

## Interactive Globe

Every official chapter appears as a glowing pin.

Pin colors indicate chapter status:

* 🟢 Active Chapter
* 🔵 Growing Chapter
* ⚪ Newly Founded
* ⚫ Dormant Chapter

Hovering over a pin displays:

* Chapter name
* City
* Country
* Status

Clicking a pin smoothly opens that chapter's profile.

---

## Chapter Profiles

Each chapter should have its own dedicated page.

Every chapter page includes:

* Chapter name
* Chapter logo (if uploaded)
* Banner image
* City & Country
* Founder
* Current Chapter Lead
* Chapter status
* Date founded
* Number of members
* Events hosted
* Students educated
* About section
* Leadership team
* Upcoming events
* Contact information
* Social links
* Gallery
* "Join This Chapter" button

---

## Starting a Chapter

Include a prominent section titled:

**Start a Chapter**

Briefly explain the process:

1. Register your interest.
2. Organize your first financial literacy event.
3. Successfully host an event with at least **10 verified attendees**.
4. Your chapter becomes officially recognized and receives a permanent location on the Vanguard map.

After approval, the organizer earns the permanent title:

**Chapter Founder**

Include a clear **Start a Chapter** button.

---

## Dormant Chapters

If a chapter becomes inactive for an extended period (for example, six months without a verified event), it enters a **Dormant** status.

Dormant chapters remain visible on the globe with gray pins.

Any volunteer can apply to revive a dormant chapter by organizing a new verified event.

After successfully reactivating the chapter, that volunteer receives the title:

**Chapter Reviver**

The original founder should always remain credited as the chapter's founder, while the reviver becomes the current chapter lead if approved.

---

## Expansion Beyond Earth

As users zoom farther out from Earth, subtly reveal the Moon, Mars, and the rest of the Solar System.

These planets are **symbolic**, representing Vanguard's vision of limitless expansion—not actual chapter locations.

Include a playful message such as:

> **Before humanity builds cities on Mars, help us build financial literacy on Earth.**

or

> **The next chapter is yours to build. Earth first. The galaxy can wait.**

The planets should serve as visual storytelling rather than interactive chapter locations.

---

## Search & Filters

Allow users to:

* Search chapters by name
* Search by country
* Search by city
* Filter by chapter status
* Sort by newest, oldest, most active, or largest

---

## Design Style

The page should feel modern, elegant, and immersive.

Use:

* Dark space background
* Realistic 3D Earth
* Soft glow effects
* Glassmorphism UI panels
* Smooth camera animations
* Responsive design for desktop and mobile

The overall experience should make visitors feel like they are exploring a living, growing global network of financial literacy leaders rather than browsing a traditional directory.
